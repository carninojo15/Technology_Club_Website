Technology Club

The Technology club is an organization that encourage Miami Dade College students to work in a Technology environment working on programming projects or improving them. Participate in STEM events and competitions representing Miami Dade College (North campus). Study career opportunities in computer-related fields, learn more about company hiring practices and job prerequisites, and to develop skills that will help in the job-search and interview processes.
